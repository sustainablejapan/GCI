What is the WCDI?

The Weighted Commitment Delivery Index is the world's first comprehensive metric for political accountability

📊

Weighted Scoring

Every government pledge is scored from 0-100% based on delivery. Scores are weighted by voter importance: Economy & Health (3×), Education & Crime (2×), Environment (1×).

🔍

Transparent Data

All data sourced from independent fact-checkers, government records, and academic research. Every claim is verifiable and documented with sources.

🌍

Global Coverage

Tracking current governments and historical data across major democracies to show long-term trends in political accountability and promise-keeping.
